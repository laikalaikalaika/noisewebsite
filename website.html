so I'm like, really into emulated modular synthesiser noise music?

Modular synth as I understand it is basically taking little 3.5mm jacks and plugging them into little bleep bloop machines built by other trans girls with ADHD, which then do something funky with the current, or control the way it behaves overall. You can make some incredible sounds with modular synth but what I do is make noise music; that is to say, create the spookiest, most unsettling, novel, and atmospheric sounds I can by sampling and using my voice, and sometimes plugging a 1970's cassette player into my computer via a MIDI converter interface and amplifer unit. It genuinely sounds kinda dope! If you want me to show you how it works sometime I'd be super happy to. xoxo - Laika
